B-Vitamin Complex
A B-vitamin blend is a combination of several B vitamins, which are essential micronutrients that play important roles in maintaining overall health and well-being. B vitamins are involved in a wide range of physiological processes such as energy production, metabolism, and the formation of red blood cells. They also support nervous system function and contribute to mental and emotional well-being.
The B-vitamin complex typically includes B1 (thiamine), B2 (riboflavin), B3 (niacin), B5 (pantothenic acid), B6 (pyridoxine), B7 (biotin), B9 (folate), and B12 (cobalamin). However, different blends may contain varying amounts of each vitamin.
